About Me

Hi! My name is Leela Mukherjee, and as a Silicon Valley native and a Computer Engineering Major at the University of Michigan, I have developed a deep interest in computer systems and hardware design. I have gone out of my way to seek out interesting experiences and projects to explore different aspects of hardware and to develop a more advanced skill set.

I have always been comfortable working in engineering labs though, and I have developed tools for companies and finished projects for my classes by tinkering with prototype boards and bench testing devices. I would love to work on the next great engineering project (I am currently looking for full-time positions starting January 2026!), and I hope my past experiences and current interests demonstrate this passion.
